About Carbon Tracker

Carbon Tracker was born from a simple idea: technology can empower people to make meaningful change. What began as a university project to understand our own carbon footprint has evolved into a platform dedicated to helping individuals and organizations measure, track, and reduce their environmental impact.

"We believe that real change starts with awareness. Our vision is to make sustainable living practical, data-driven, and personal."

The Carbon Tracker Team

Our mission is to help everyone—from students to small businesses—track their environmental impact, make better choices, and contribute to a cleaner planet.
